Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Multiplication Table Practice App (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Idan Shanny (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information from users. All data generated by the Application is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Stored Locally

The Application stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Practice session results (correct and incorrect answers)

This data is stored solely on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the Application.

Third Party Access

The Application does not share any information with third parties. No data is transmitted from your device to any external servers or services.

Children's Privacy

The Application is designed for children and does not collect any personal information from children or any other users. Parents can be confident that no personal data is collected, stored externally, or shared.

Security

Since the Application does not collect or transmit any personal information, there are no security concerns related to data transmission or storage on external servers. All data remains on your device under your control.
